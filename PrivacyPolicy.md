<h1>Privacy Policy</h1>
<p class="subtitle">DailyQ &middot; Effective date: February 21, 2026</p>

<h2>1. Developer Information</h2>
<p>
    DailyQ ("the App") is developed and maintained by
    <strong>Denis Valerievich Maslakov</strong> (individual developer, Russian Federation).
    In this policy, "we", "us", and "our" refer to the developer.
</p>

<h2>2. Overview</h2>
<p>
    The App is a private, offline-first journaling application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.
    This Privacy Policy explains what data the App processes, how it is stored, and what rights you have.
    By installing or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
</p>

<h2>3. Data Storage</h2>
<p>
    All your data — including journal answers, app settings, preferences, and notification schedules —
    is stored <strong>exclusively on your device</strong> using the operating system's local storage mechanisms.
    The App does not transmit, upload, or sync any data to external servers, cloud services, or third parties.
</p>

<h2>4. Data We Do NOT Collect</h2>
<p>The App does not collect, process, or transmit any of the following:</p>
<ul>
    <li>Personal information (name, email, phone number, or any account credentials)</li>
    <li>Location data (GPS, Wi-Fi, or cell-tower based)</li>
    <li>Device identifiers, advertising IDs (IDFA/GAID), or hardware fingerprints</li>
    <li>Usage analytics, behavioral data, or session recordings</li>
    <li>Crash reports or diagnostics sent to remote servers</li>
    <li>Photos, contacts, calendar, microphone, or camera data</li>
</ul>

<h2>5. Device Permissions</h2>
<p>
    The App may request the following device permission:
</p>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Notifications</strong> — to send local reminders about daily questions.
    This permission is optional; the App is fully functional without it.
    Notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any remote servers
    or push notification services (APNs/FCM).</li>
</ul>
<p>The App does not request access to your camera, microphone, photos, contacts, location, or any other device capabilities.</p>

<h2>6. Third-Party Services</h2>
<p>
    The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or social media services.
    No SDKs that collect or transmit user data are included. No data is shared with third parties.
</p>

<h2>7. Data Security</h2>
<p>
    Since all data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security measures,
    including filesystem sandboxing, device encryption (when enabled), and screen lock authentication.
    We do not have access to your data at any point, as it never leaves your device.
</p>

<h2>8. Data Deletion and Retention</h2>
<p>
    You have full control over your data at all times:
</p>
<ul>
    <li>You can delete all your data through the <strong>Settings</strong> screen within the App.</li>
    <li>Uninstalling the App will permanently remove all locally stored data from your device.</li>
</ul>
<p>
    We do not retain any copies of your data, as it is never transmitted off your device.
</p>

<h2>9. Children's Privacy</h2>
<p>
    The App is not specifically directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction).
    However, since the App does not collect any personal data whatsoever, it complies with the
    Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations.
    No personal information is collected from any user, regardless of age.
</p>

<h2>10. Your Rights</h2>
<p>
    Because the App does not collect or store any personal data on external servers, traditional data subject
    rights (access, rectification, portability, erasure) are inherently fulfilled — your data exists only
    on your device and is entirely under your control. You may view, modify, or delete your data at any time
    directly within the App.
</p>
<p>
    If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you are entitled to the rights provided under the
    General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since no personal data is collected or processed by us,
    there is no data to access, export, or request deletion of from our side.
</p>

<h2>11. International Users</h2>
<p>
    The App is available globally. Since all data remains on your device and is never transferred to any server,
    no cross-border data transfers occur. Your data stays in the jurisdiction where your device is physically located.
</p>

<h2>12. Changes to This Policy</h2>
<p>
    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with
    an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of the App
    after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
</p>

<h2>13. Contact</h2>
<p>
    If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
